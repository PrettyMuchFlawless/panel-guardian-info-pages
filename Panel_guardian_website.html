Temperature Difference

The system continuously compares the temperature inside your electrical panel to the room temperature. A significant difference indicates a potential problem like a bad breaker or loose wiring, which requires immediate attention.

Current Difference

This feature measures the electrical current between the power wires. A healthy panel has a difference of zero. A non-zero reading can signal electricity leaking or returning through the ground wire, indicating faulty equipment or incorrect wiring.

The Panel Guardian uses these readings to calculate an Alert Factor from 0 to 4, giving you a quick and easy way to understand the health of your electrical system.

Stay Alerted, Stay Safe

Hardwired Alarm

The system activates relay contacts to notify your main controller, barn monitoring system, or farm security system, ensuring your entire operation is aware of the issue.

Monitored Alarm

Receive direct alerts via email or text message, allowing you to take immediate action no matter where you are.

Once alerted, you can call your electrician to find and fix the problem before it leads to bigger issues!
