How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your account and associated data.

1

Navigate to Account Settings

Open the app and locate the **Settings** tab. This is the gear icon in the bottom right hand corner.

2

Confirm Account Deletion

Within the settings menu, find the **Delete Account** button.

Important:

Deleting your account is permanent and will remove all associated data, including progress, purchased items, and profile information.
