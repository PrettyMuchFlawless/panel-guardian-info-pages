Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Panel Guardian ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information in connection with your use of the Panel Guardian mobile application ("the App").

We take your privacy very seriously. Our app is designed to provide you with monitoring and control over your connected panel system without collecting unnecessary personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you through the app itself. The app is a local interface for your panel monitoring system and operates primarily on a local network.

The app may require access to your local network to communicate with your Panel Guardian system. This access is used solely for the purpose of app functionality, and no data is transmitted to us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

The information processed by the app (e.g., panel status, sensor readings, and control commands) is used only to display information to you and to send commands to your local panel system. This data is not stored or analyzed by us.

3. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the data processed by the app. All communication between the app and your Panel Guardian system occurs on your local network. No data is stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5. Your Consent

By using the Panel Guardian app, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.
