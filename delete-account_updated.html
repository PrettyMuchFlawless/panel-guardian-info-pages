Account Deletion Steps

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

We are working on deleting the account. Please follow the steps below.

1. Submit Your Username

Enter the username associated with the account you want to delete and press the account deletion button above.

2. Verify Your Request

Make sure the username entered is correct. Incorrect usernames may delay or prevent processing of your deletion request.

3. Watch for Follow-Up Contact

In some cases, we may need to verify that you are the account owner before continuing with deletion.

4. Understand the Consequences

Deleting your account may permanently remove access to account data, settings, alerts, device associations, and related records.

Some information may be retained where required by law, security, fraud prevention, or operational recordkeeping.

5. Do Not Create Another Account Yet

Please do not create another account while your deletion request is being reviewed or processed.

6. Contact Us if Needed

If you submitted the deletion request in error or need help with the process, contact us as soon as possible.
