SMS Notifications - Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

1. Service Description

Smart Ag Solutions Inc ("Smart Ag," "we," "us," or "our") provides SMS text message notifications to all customers who purchase Panel Guardian electrical panel monitoring devices. SMS alerts are an integral part of the Panel Guardian service and are automatically enabled upon device purchase and registration.

2. Automatic Enrollment and Consent

By purchasing a Panel Guardian device and providing your mobile phone number during registration, you automatically consent to receive SMS text messages from Smart Ag Solutions Inc.

This SMS notification service is included with your Panel Guardian device and ensures you receive critical alerts about your device status. Your purchase and device registration constitute your express written consent to receive automated text messages at the phone number you provide.

3. Types of Messages

You will automatically receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Device Anomaly Alerts: Notifications when your Panel Guardian device detects abnormal electrical metrics (amperage, temperature, or alert factors)
  • Offline Notifications: Alerts when your device loses connectivity or stops transmitting data

4. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your device activity and status. You may receive multiple messages per day during device events or no messages during normal operation. We do not send marketing or promotional messages through this service.

5. Costs and Charges

Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan. Smart Ag Solutions Inc does not charge fees for SMS notifications, but you are responsible for any charges from your wireless carrier. Contact your carrier for details about your messaging plan.

6. Opt-Out Instructions

While SMS notifications are included with your Panel Guardian service, you may opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from our service
  • Contacting support at support@smartagsolutionsinc.ca

Note: Opting out of SMS notifications may reduce the effectiveness of your Panel Guardian monitoring system, as you will not receive real-time alerts about device issues.

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You may re-enable notifications at any time through your Panel Guardian account settings.

7. Help and Support

For assistance with SMS notifications, reply HELP to any message or contact our support team at support@smartagsolutionsinc.ca.

8. Supported Carriers and Geographic Areas

This SMS service is available to customers with mobile phone numbers in the United States and Canada. Supported carriers include all major wireless providers. Message delivery may vary by carrier and location.

9. Privacy and Data Use

Your mobile phone number is stored securely and used solely for delivering Panel Guardian service notifications. We do not:

  • Share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Send promotional or advertising messages through this service
  • Sell or rent your contact information

For complete privacy practices, see our Privacy Policy.

10. Device and Service Requirements

To receive SMS notifications, you must:

  • Provide a valid mobile phone number during Panel Guardian device registration
  • Maintain an active mobile phone number with a supported carrier
  • Have sufficient cellular coverage to receive text messages
  • Keep your contact information current in your Panel Guardian account

11. Delivery and Reliability

While we strive for reliable message delivery, Smart Ag Solutions Inc cannot guarantee:

  • Timely delivery of all SMS messages

SMS notifications are informational alerts and should not be relied upon as the sole means of monitoring your electrical panel. Critical safety monitoring requires additional safeguards.
