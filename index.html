Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect the app to my panel?

You can connect the app by navigating to the "Settings" menu and entering the IP address and login credentials for your Panel Guardian system. Make sure both your phone and the panel are on the same network.

What should I do if the app is not connecting?

First, ensure your phone has an active internet connection. Next, check if your Panel Guardian system is powered on and connected to the network. You can also try restarting both the app and your panel.
